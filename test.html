Welcome to our admin Page

This is MULTIPURPOSE APPLICATIONThis application is being designed keeping in mind the size of our campus , the formalities required to complaint and to carter the students daily needs much more efficiently . Our app will be student centric ,the app gives students in VIT university to interact with the authorities easily. It will have 3 modules namely Hostel , Mess , Laundry .Every module will be having an individual admin , complaint box and some basic features that a student needs. To make the formalities less we will have every possible feature by which student can interact with the warden/ mess incharge /Laundry person directly and the admin will be designed to take necessary actions.

MESS

Most of the students face problem in deciding whether to go to mess for the meal or not because of their food preferences .The admin of mess would update the time table so the students can see it.

HOSTEL

This is the most important module of this application this would have the following features. 2.1 Request Electrician We could just click a button and request for an electrician in case of power failure. 2.2 Complaint Then comes the complaint of food , we personally face a lot of problem while complaining in the hostel because we have to do a lot of paper work.

LAUNDARY

Timings of Laundry shop Most of the time the shop is closed so it would be very convenient for us to know when would it be open .
